Typographic Poster

This projcet was created for Typography 1 at Oregon State University. The goal of the projcet was to design a poster that incorporated the provided text.

With this project, my goal was to create a poster that shows the given set of information in a way that demonstrates good hierarchy while also being eye catching.

I limited myself to using simple, recognizable shapes to create the design. I wanted to base the design on an easily recognizable form. Since the poster is meant to represent a lecture series during sprite time, I chose to base the design off of a tree. Trees, and fresh greens, are often associated with springtime.
